!!! Hello World Example Page

!! Test the HelloWorld-TagHandler:

{{{
[{KnowWEPlugin helloworld}]
}}}

renders as:

[{KnowWEPlugin helloworld}]

----

Further, a ''count'' variable can be specified:

{{{
[{KnowWEPlugin helloworld , count = 5 }]
}}}

rendering as:
[{KnowWEPlugin helloworld , count = 5 }]

----

!! Test the HelloKnowWE-Type:

{{{
Hello KnowWE!
}}}

renders as:
Hello KnowWE!
